Common questions

Quick answers

Why can I not register for an event?

Registration requires a published, upcoming event with at least one place available.

When can I leave a review?

Reviews become available after an event ends and only to users who registered for it.

Where do organizers manage events?

Log in with an Organizer account and open Manage events from the navbar.
